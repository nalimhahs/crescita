TKM College of Engineering, Kollam

Esteemed as the first Government Aided College in Kerala, the second most sought after destination for engineering aspirants across the state, TKM College of Engineering bears a transcendent acclaim with a plethora of academic excellence and ethos. Having its foundation laid by Dr. Rajendra Prasad, the erstwhile President of India in 1956, the college continuous to effectuate the founder Janab Thangal Kunju Musaliar’s ideology of transforming the entire state’s educational aspiration by equipping the students with the best engineering techniques and instilling in them a sense of responsibility.
