College Representative Program


Do you think you have the drive and charisma to take charge and lead your peers? Then get ready to become the College Representative for Crescita '20. This January, you'll have the golden chance to be the voice of Crescita in your college and bring in crowds to experience and explore. The program showering benefits from appreciation CRP (college representative program) certificates, extra coupons with all other beneficiaries, to free registrations for competitive events and intellectual workshops. Barge in and grab them all if you think YOU are the ONE !!

As a CR, you will:
  • Act as a point of contact for Crescita '20 in your college, and inform your peers about our events and activities through various means, including Social Media.
  • Ensure maximum participation from your college in workshops and competitions, and help them to inquire about event details.


One will be called a successful CR if he/she can bring at least 10 participants during the fest.

Incentives
  1. Certification of Appreciation awarded on completion of the College Representative Program.
  2. Attractive goodies and all beneficiary coupons
  3. CRs with atleast 10 participants avails free event passes and  20+ participants avails a free workshop pass.
  4. Free accommodation provided for the above mentioned CRs.
  5. Reimbursement of one-way sleeper ticket for CRs bringing 20+ participants.


So what are you waiting for?

Join us on 18th and 19th of January 2020
